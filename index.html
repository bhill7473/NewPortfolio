A Little About Me...

I'm a developer that recently graduated from Trilogy's Case Western Reserve's coding boot camp. I am currently a TA and a Tutor for the same boot camp. I am pursuing a BS in Digital Sciences at Kent State University. I got interested in programming while trying to figure out on my own how the internet works, and just playing around with different technologies computers have. Since then I got serious about really learning how everything works inside, and out. This led me to the path I'm on now. I enjoy web developing because you can turn your ideas into reality, and in a sense your a modern day artist. When I'm not coding I like to workout, listen to music, play video games, watch movies, and do pretty much anything outdoors.
